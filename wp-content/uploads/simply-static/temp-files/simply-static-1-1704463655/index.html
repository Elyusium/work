Introducing Ourselves.

PL International is a leading construction equipment company that specializes in providing high-quality machinery to various industries. The company was established in 2016 and has since grown to become one of the most reputable providers of construction equipment globally. We offers an extensive range of brands and models of new construction equipment to suit every need. We also provide maintenance and repair services and are committed to delivering outstanding customer service. We have a highly skilled and dedicated team committed to providing innovative solutions and going above and beyond to meet their customers’ expectations.
